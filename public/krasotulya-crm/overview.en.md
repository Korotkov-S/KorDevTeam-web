# Krasotulya-CRM: feature overview

Krasotulya-CRM is a system to manage customers, deals, and team tasks in one place.

## What’s inside

- Pipeline and deals
- Customer database (contacts/companies)
- Tasks and reminders
- Comments and activity history
- Sales reports

## Who it’s for

- Beauty salons and service businesses
- Small and mid-size teams with a sales pipeline
- Teams that need to track communication and never lose leads
